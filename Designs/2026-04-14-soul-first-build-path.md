## Vision

### 10x Check
A living, self-improving value investing intelligence that accumulates wisdom across years, develops its own original investment theses, and creates a publicly verifiable track record that becomes the definitive proof that AI can do long-term value investing better than 99% of humans.

The masters provide the foundation. The system EVOLVES beyond them. It's not a Buffett simulator. It's a new value investor that learned from Buffett, then grew through its own experience.

### Platonic Ideal
A Bloomberg terminal for long-term value investors, operated by AI that thinks like the best investors who ever lived. You check it once a week. When it says "buy," you trust the reasoning enough to buy. When it says "hold through this drawdown," you trust it enough to hold. The system has EARNED your trust through consistent, explainable reasoning.

Key insight from the founder: "保持谦逊 — nobody should claim to know all the decision standards before doing the homework. The soul packaging IS the process of discovering investment criteria." The standards emerge from deep study, they aren't defined upfront.

### Critical Correction from Founder
AI (including Claude) defaults to equity research patterns (conviction scoring, quarterly updates) instead of genuine value investing patterns. Value investors don't update conviction quarterly. They research deeply, decide, and hold. The soul packaging exists precisely to prevent this pattern contamination.
